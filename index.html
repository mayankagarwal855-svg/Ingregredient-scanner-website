About CleanLabel

CleanLabel analyses food ingredient lists against a built-in database of food additives, E-numbers, preservatives, colours, sweeteners and common ingredients — all offline, no internet required for analysis.

Tap Type / paste ingredients on the Scan screen for instant offline analysis. The photo and barcode options require an internet connection.

How ratings work

Safe (green) — Generally healthy, no known concerns at normal food intake levels.
Caution (amber) — Some research raises concerns, or safe only in moderation, or known to cause reactions in sensitive individuals.
Avoid (red) — Classified as harmful, carcinogenic, or banned in multiple countries.

Disclaimer

CleanLabel is for general information only and does not constitute medical or dietary advice. Ratings are based on publicly available research and regulatory information. Always consult a healthcare professional for specific dietary needs or allergies.
